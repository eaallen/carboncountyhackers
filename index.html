Hackathon Information

Everything you need to know about the Carbon County Hackathon.

Event Details

Date: January 20-21, 2025
Duration: 48 hours
Location: Carbon County Community Center
Team Size: 1-4 participants
Prize Pool: $10,000 total

What to Expect

  • Networking opportunities with local developers and tech professionals
  • Workshops on cutting-edge technologies and best practices
  • Free meals, snacks, and beverages throughout the event
  • Access to mentors and industry experts
  • Prizes for multiple categories including innovation and technical excellence
  • Opportunity to showcase your project to potential employers
